How to Use Blox Fruits Scripts

Step 1: Get an Executor

You need a script executor to run Blox Fruits scripts. Popular options include Synapse X, KRNL, or Fluxus.

Step 2: Download and Install

Download your chosen executor from its official website and follow the installation instructions.

Step 3: Whitelist (if needed)

Some executors require you to whitelist them in your antivirus or Windows Defender to function properly.

Step 1: Launch Roblox

Start Roblox and join a Blox Fruits game server.

Step 2: Open Your Executor

Launch your script executor and make sure it's injected into Roblox.

Step 3: Copy and Paste Script

Copy the script you want to use from our extension, paste it into your executor, and click Execute.

Script Not Working

Make sure your executor is updated to the latest version and try rejoining the game.

Getting Kicked from Server

Some scripts may be detected by anti-cheat. Try using a different script or joining a different server.

Executor Not Injecting

Restart your executor as administrator or check if Roblox has been updated recently.
